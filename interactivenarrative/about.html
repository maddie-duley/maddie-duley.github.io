About Me

Maddie Duley is a designer pursuing a career in UI/UX. As a third-year design student at the University of California, Davis, Maddie has had the opportunity to design app and website layouts, create digital booklets, and work on a range of other design projects. Maddie is passionate about creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that enhance the overall user experience. In her free time, she enjoys exploring new design trends and learning new design tools.
