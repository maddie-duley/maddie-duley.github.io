Senior Spot

Challange:

Retired seniors often struggle to build a sense of community, feel connected, and have opportunities to meet new peers and explore new interests. I believe that by developing an informative website with design choices tailored to the target audience, incorporating UX research and visually appealing UI, I can create a website prototype for both desktop and mobile platforms that facilitates connections among local seniors.

Solution:

Through a comprehensive four-step design process, I have developed four website mock-ups for mobile and desktop, showcasing the effective application of UI/UX principles with careful consideration for an elderly audience. By creating personas, and wireframes, and conducting thorough website research and comparative analysis, I have successfully designed website prototypes that prioritize information architecture and incorporate valuable feedback received from critiques.
